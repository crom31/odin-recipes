Homemade Pizza Recipe

Homemade pizza

This homemade pizza recipe is perfect for parties. Everyone loves pizza!

Quick, easy and delicious. This recipe produces a mouthwatering classic.

Ingredients

  • 335ml warm water
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 490g bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspon sugar
  • 12 ounces tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 garlic powder

Steps

  1. For sauce: combine all sauce ingredients with 1/2 cup water in a medium bowl.
  2. For crusts: combine 2 cups of flour with the dry yeast,sugar and salt. Add the water and oil and mix until well blended. Gradually add enough remaining flour slowly, until a soft, sticky dough ball is formed
  3. Knead for about 4 minutes, on a floured surface, until dough is smooth and elastic. Add more flour, if needed.
  4. Divide dough in half. Pat each half into a 12-inch greased pizza pan
  5. For pizzas: preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Top crusts with sauce and cheese
  6. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly.
