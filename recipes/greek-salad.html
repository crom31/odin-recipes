Greek Salad Recipe

Greek Salad

This is an incredible good Greek salad, nice and tangy even better in the summer when you use fresh vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 head romain letucce-rinsed, dried and chopped
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the Romaine, onion, olives, bell peppers, tomatoes, cucumber and cheese.
  2. Whisk together the olive oil and oregano.
  3. Pour dressing over salad, toss and serve.
