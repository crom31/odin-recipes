BLT Sandwich Recipe

BLT Sandwich

The basic classic: bacon, lettuce and tomato... nothing fancy, just delicious.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bacon
  • 2 leaves lettuces
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 2 slices bread, toasted
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon in a large, deep skillet over medium-high until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain the bacon slices on a paper towel-lined plate
  2. Arrange the cooked bacon, lettuce, and tomato slices on one slice of bread. Spread one side of remaining bread slice with mayonnaise.
  3. Bring the two pieces together to make the sandwich.
